Our Attorneys Team

Kolawole Suleiman Lawal: LL B; LL M; BL (Solicitor & Advocate; Notary Public).

He studied at the University of Lagos (UNILAG), where he earned LL B (Hons) in 1991. He attended the Nigerian Law School and was called to the Nigerian Bar in 1992.
He studied at the University of Ibadan (UI), where he attained a Master of Law Degree (LL M) in 2003 with specialization in Commercial Law.
He is a member of the Chartered Institute of Taxation of Nigeria. He is a member of the Chartered Institute of Arbitration, United Kingdom.
He was appointed a Notary Public in 2002. He was a Partner in the Law Firm of Ayinde Sanni & Co. a well-established commercial law firm in Lagos, Nigeria until 2017.
