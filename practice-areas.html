Criminal Law

Defence of criminal charges in all the Courts and Tribunals in Nigeria. The Nigerian Constitution presumes that a person accused of a crime is innocent and the prosecutor must prove that the defendant committed the crime with intent, and beyond a reasonable doubt.
We can assist our clients in the application for bail and defence. We will ensure protection of our client’s rights in the criminal justice process and administration, including arrest, arraignment, pleas, trial, evidence, motions, appeals, and post trial remedies.
